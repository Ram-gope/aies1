THE BLUEPRINT

MANDATORY 12-POINT PITCH DECK STRUCTURE

01

Intro & Hook

Name of the Startup/Product and the "Hook" (One-line elevator pitch).

02

Problem & Solution

What is the pain point? How does your tech solve it specifically?

03

Innovation (X-Factor)

What makes this different from existing manual methods? What is your value add?

04

Market Research

Data/Charts showing the need for this product. (e.g., "60% of students struggle with X").

05

Audience & Strategy

Who will use it? (Gen-Z, Corporate HR, etc). How will you market to them?

06

Competitor Analysis

SWOT Analysis. Who else is doing this and why will users choose YOU?

07

Tech Architecture

Tech Stack (React, AWS, Python). Does the team have these skills currently?

08

Team Intro

Roles: CEO, CTO, CMO. Highlight the 3-5 key members.

09

Experience

Have the members built anything before? Projects, Internships, Small Ventures.

10

Financials

Cost to build: Server costs, API fees, Marketing budget.

11

Breakeven Point

The "Money Slide": How many users/subscriptions do you need to recover investment?

12

Future Scale-Up

Roadmap for the next 6 months to 1 year.
